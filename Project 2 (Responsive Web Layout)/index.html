🍕 Our Story

A Passion Born in Naples, Perfected Here

Pizza Point was founded in 2005 by Chef Antonio Rossi, who brought authentic Neapolitan pizza-making traditions from his family’s 100-year-old bakery in Naples, Italy. What began as a small corner shop with a single wood-fired oven has grown into the city’s most beloved pizzeria.

We believe every pizza should be a masterpiece — crafted with hand-tossed dough, the freshest ingredients, and baked at exactly the right temperature for that perfect golden crust.
